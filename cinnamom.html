CINNAMON ROLLS

Description

A perfect choice for that sweet-tooth. A dish you can never go wrong with.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking powder and salt.
  2. Cut-in the shortening.
  3. Add the milk, stirring with a knife until the dough is soft and slightly sticky.
  4. Knead the dough until it "springs back" when you push your hand into it.
  5. Roll the dough into a 9" by 13" (23cm by 30cm) rectangle. Use a 9" by 13" (23cm by 30cm) baking pan as a mould for the size.
  6. Brush butter over the dough, leaving about ¼" (0.75cm) around the edges clear.
  7. Combine brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon and salt. Sprinkle on buttered dough, leaving about ¼" around the edges clear.
  8. Press the sugar mixture down. Roll the dough up lengthwise into a log, as tight as you can.
  9. Trim the edges, cut into eight pieces.
  10. Arrange the buns in a small pan, so that all the buns are touching.
  11. Bake at 425°F (210˚C) for 15-20 minutes.
